Modular Dungeon

Im made components for a modular dungeon

First I made a barrel for the dungeon. This is a cilinder with some extrusion and some bevels to make it look a little bit old.
Than I made a crate. This is a box with some inlines and extrusion. Also some bevel to make it also look old.
I also made 3 different pillars so there is some variation. Two pillars are made of an box and one is made of an cilinder. These I made with extrusions and also with some bevels.
