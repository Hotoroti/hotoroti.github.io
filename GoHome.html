Go Home

My first 3d game

Go home was a school project when I was in my second year of college. This was also the first time I used unity so I needed to learn fast. Go home is a 3rd person strategy based hack and slash tower defense game where you as the captain must go from planet to planet to collect resources and to get back to your home planet.
